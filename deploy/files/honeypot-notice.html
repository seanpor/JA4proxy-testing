Research Honeypot Notice

This server is a research honeypot. It is not a production service. If you believe you reached it by accident, you are in the wrong place and no action is required on your part.

What this server does

It records TLS handshake fingerprints (JA4), source IP address, and HTTP request metadata from clients that connect to it. The form on the landing page is decorative: submitted values are logged and immediately discarded. No account, session, or personal record is created.

What this server does not do

  • It does not offer a real product, service, or API.
  • It does not sell, forward, or trade collected data.
  • It does not issue credentials, authenticate users, or store passwords.

Lawful basis (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f))

Processing of connection metadata is carried out under the legitimate interest of security research into TLS-fingerprintable bot and attacker behaviour. A balancing assessment is available on request.

Retention

Connection logs and metrics are retained for up to 90 days and then deleted automatically by the log pipeline.
