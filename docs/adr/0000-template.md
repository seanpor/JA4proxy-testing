# NNNN. Short title of the decision

## Status

Accepted | Proposed | Deprecated | Superseded by `NNNN-short-title.md`

## Context

What is the issue we're addressing? What forces are at play (technical,
business, project)? Describe the situation as it exists *before* the
decision so a future reader can understand why the decision had to be
made at all. Avoid editorialising — the context is neutral.

## Decision

The change we're making, stated as a direct, active-voice sentence.
Follow with any supporting detail: specific tools, versions, roles,
templates, or files that encode the decision.

## Consequences

What becomes easier or harder as a result? List both positive and
negative outcomes, plus any follow-on work the decision forces. If the
decision has been superseded, note which ADR replaced it and why.
